Variable Description
---------------------
Variables used in the "run_analysis.r" code:

tr_x:data frame containg information of "x_train.txt"
tr_xl:data frame containg information of "y_train.txt"(label information)
tr_xs:data frame containg information of "subject_train.txt"
tr   :data frame containg information of entire train data set
ts_x :data frame containg information of "x_test.txt"
ts_xl:data frame containg information of "y_test.txt"
ts_xs:data frame containg information of "subject_test.txt"
ts   :data frame containg information of entire test data set
data :data frame containg information of entire test and train data sets(step 1 of the project)
tr_f :data frame containg information of "features.txt"
l1   :data frame containg information of variables based on measurement of mean function
l2   :data frame containg information of variables based on measurement of standard deviation function
tf   :data frame containg information of variables based on measurement of both mean and std functions
ext_data:data frame containg only values of the required variables.(step 2 of the project)
t_l  :data frame containg information of "activity_labels.txt"
labeld_data:data frame containg information of variables along with the activities(step 3 of the project)
